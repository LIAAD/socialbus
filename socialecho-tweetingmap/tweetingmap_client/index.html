Tweeting Map is an experimental visualization of near-realtime public geo-localized tweets.

Each circle represents a tweet on its location, at near the moment it is being posted. The color comes from user’s profile, and the size represents the number of user’s followers.

Tweeting Map may be run on one of the following modes:

Fireworks mode Paint mode

Open on your smartphone
